About Me

Hi, I'm Grant! I'm an undergrad at BU, and I double major in physics and math. I intend to go to grad school in physics, and I'm mostly interested in mathematical QFT and high-energy physics. I also love math, and am currently very interested in higher category theory. My interests in math change every 2-3 months though, but in general I'm interested in some of the more abstract areas of math.

I spend a lot of my free time learning math and physics. I genuinely love learning about things I'm interested in, and will happily sit down and work through a textbook for fun. I also have very ambitious reading plans for the next two years.

I'm also very fluent in LaTeX, and have created my own package to manage my title pages, and have many different templates for my notes and assignments. I also have a suite of scripts that streamline my LaTeX workflow - both for note-taking and homework assignments. I've also coded my own Minecraft mod, which is fittingly named "good mod", and I use arch (btw).
